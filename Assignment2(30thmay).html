A Hassle-Free Tutorial on How to Bake a Cake

Ingredients

  1. All Purpose Flour
  2. Butter
  3. Oil
  4. Eggs
  5. Baking Powder
  6. Baking Soda
  7. Vanilla Essence
  8. Sugar

Pre-Requisites

  1. A Microwave Oven
  2. Lining the Pan (with Butter or Parchment Paper)
  3. A Mixer
  4. A Sieve (optional, to sift the dry ingredients)

Procedure

  1. Add All Purpose Flour, Baking Powder and Baking Soda in a bowl.
  2. In the mixer, add Butter and Sugar, and beat it well.
  3. Add the eggs one by one while mixing it at a high speed.
  4. Add the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients and mix at low speed.
  5. Add vanilla essence and mix it in.
  6. Then, add oil and mix it well.
  7. Pour the mixture into the lined pan and bake it at 180 degrees for 12 to 15 minutes.
  8. Let it cool. Then, cut into pieces and enjoy...

Tip: It is good to sift the dry ingredients thoroughly so that no lumps are formed in the batter.
